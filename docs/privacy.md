# Lorekeeper — Privacy Policy

_Last updated: 2026-04-29_

## Summary

Lorekeeper does **not** collect, transmit, or sell any of your personal data.
Everything you do in the app — your library, citations, characters, notes,
links, dictionary entries, reading history, and reader settings — stays on
your device. There is no account, no sign-in, no cloud sync, and no
telemetry. Lorekeeper has no servers.

## Data we store on your device

The following data is created by you and saved locally inside the app's
private storage:

- **Library** — files of books you import or scan from device storage.
- **Reading state** — current chapter, page, and progress for each book.
- **Annotations** — citations, notes, character entries (names, aliases,
  descriptions, affiliations), dictionary entries, and cross-book links.
- **Reader preferences** — font, theme, layout, and selection-menu order.
- **Reading statistics** — timestamps and approximate word counts for the
  pages you turn while reading.

This data never leaves your device unless **you** explicitly export it
(via Backup & restore, or by sharing a Book Bundle).

## Permissions

- **Storage / Photos & Media access** — required so you can pick book files
  from your device to import into the library, and so the app can save
  exported backups and bundles to your `Download/` folder.
- **Internet access** — used for two purposes: (1) tapping the **Translate**
  action on selected text opens Google Translate in your browser, and
  (2) book covers embedded inside EPUB/Kindle files may be referenced over
  the network only when the file itself includes such a reference. The app
  never sends your annotations, library contents, or reading data over
  the network.

The app does not request access to your contacts, location, microphone,
camera, calendar, or any other personal information.

## Third-party services

- **Google Translate** — when you tap the **Translate** action on a
  selected passage, the selected text is passed to Google Translate's web
  interface (translate.google.com) via your default browser. This happens
  only when you explicitly tap that action. Google's privacy policy
  applies once you leave Lorekeeper.

No analytics SDKs, no advertising SDKs, no crash-reporting services, no
fingerprinting libraries, and no other third parties are bundled with
the app.

## Children's privacy

Lorekeeper is not directed to children under 13 and does not knowingly
collect data from anyone, regardless of age.

## Data export, retention, and deletion

You can export all your data at any time via **Backup & restore** in the
app's drawer. You can delete all your data by uninstalling the app —
Android removes the app's private storage on uninstall.

## Changes to this policy

If material changes are ever made to how the app handles data, this page
will be updated and the "Last updated" date above will change. The
canonical version of this policy lives in the project repository at
`docs/privacy.md` so its history is publicly auditable in git.
